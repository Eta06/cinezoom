# Privacy Policy for CineZoom for YT™

**Last updated:** December 23, 2025

**CineZoom for YT™** ("the Extension") is designed with your privacy in mind. We believe that your data belongs to you.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any sensitive personal data.**

*   **No User Accounts:** The Extension does not require an account and does not request any personal information (such as email, name, or phone number).
*   **No Tracking:** We do not track your browsing history, search queries, or video viewing habits.
*   **No Analytics:** We do not use third-party analytics services (like Google Analytics) within the Extension.
*   **No Server Communication:** The Extension runs entirely locally on your device. No data is sent to external servers.

## Permissions

The Extension requires the following permissions to function:

*   **`storage`**: Used solely to save your preferred zoom level settings locally on your browser. This data never leaves your device.
*   **`activeTab` / `scripting`**: Used to modify the styling (CSS) of the YouTube video player to apply the ultrawide effect. We do not read or store the content of the pages you visit.
*   **Host Permissions (`*://*.youtube.com/*`)**: Required to activate the extension functionality specifically on YouTube.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify users through an update to the Extension.
